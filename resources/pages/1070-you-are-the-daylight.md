Fifth. *You are the daylight.*

The voice begins a series of identifications. *You are the daylight.* You are not just lit by it; you *are* it. The light that fills the world is the same substance as you. There is no separation between the source of illumination and the one being illuminated. You are made of the daylight. You are an expression of it. You are how the daylight, in some sense, looks at itself.

This is an extension of the line earlier in the poem about being *information from a star*. The light from the sun that produced your atoms is still in you. You are made of compressed and reorganized daylight. The voice is collapsing a distinction. *You are the daylight.*
