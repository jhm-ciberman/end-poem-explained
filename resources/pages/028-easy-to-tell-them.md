The voice that almost broke discipline a moment ago is still there, still wanting. It has not let go of the longing. *It would be so easy.* One sentence. One revelation. One unveiling. The universe could tell you what you are in a breath, and the long dream of confusion could end.

The temptation is real. The voice is admitting it. There is a part of the cosmic perspective that wants to just *say it*.

But notice the ellipsis — *to tell them...* The sentence trails. The voice can imagine telling you and it can almost feel the relief of having told you, and then it stops itself. It does not finish the sentence because to finish the sentence would be to do the thing.
