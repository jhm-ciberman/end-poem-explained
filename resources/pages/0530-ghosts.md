The lingering dead. The presences in the old house. The thing that touches you on the back of the neck when you are alone in the corridor. Across cultures, across centuries, humans have reported the felt presence of the no-longer-living, and the voice claims that too. *Ghosts. Yes. Us.*

Whether you read this literally or psychologically — whether you think ghosts are spirits or projections of grief — the poem includes both readings. The felt experience of being haunted is one of the ways the universe has reached people, and the universe is acknowledging the form.
