Eighth. The mirror of the previous line. *The light you seek is within you.*

You have been seeking light. You have been looking for clarity, for guidance, for the answer, for the meaning, for the right path. You have been looking *outward* for it. The voice tells you: *it is inside you*. The light you seek is not in the next book, the next teacher, the next relationship, the next accomplishment. It is in you. You only need to find it.

Together, the seventh and eighth lines deliver the same teaching with two faces. The dark is in you. The light is in you. The whole drama is internal. You are not waiting for an external rescue. You are not threatened by an external enemy. You contain everything. The work is to find it.
