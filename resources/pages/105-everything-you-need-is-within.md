And the third. *Everything you need is within you.*

This is the most ancient spiritual claim in the world, delivered without ornament. Every contemplative tradition arrives at it eventually. The kingdom of God is within you. Atman is Brahman. Buddha-nature is your original face. The treasure is in your own field; you only need to dig.

The voice is telling you that you are not lacking. You are not waiting on something external — a teacher, a relationship, an achievement, a piece of information, a permission — to become whole. The completeness is already inside you. *Everything you need is within you.*

This is also the universe gently dismantling the tendency to seek the answer outside. The voice could have said *come to us* or *call out and we will help you*. It does not. It says — *the resources are inside you. Look there.* The universe is sending you back to yourself. With confidence. With certainty. *You already have what you need.*
