The voice now poses the question that the entire poem has been building toward. *Why does the universe touch your skin, and throw light on you?*

Take this question seriously for a moment. *Why* does it? You could give a physical answer — because there are atmospheric molecules bumping against your skin, because photons are emitted by light sources and reflected by surfaces. That is the *mechanism*. The voice is asking about the *purpose*.

And the framing implies that there *is* a purpose. The contact is intentional. The light-throwing is *for something*. The universe is not absentmindedly bumping into you. It is doing it on purpose. The question is: what is the purpose?
