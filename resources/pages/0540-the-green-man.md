The pagan European spirit of the wilderness. The face peering out of the leaves in cathedral carvings, smuggled into the stones by craftsmen who would not let the old gods go. The fertility of the forest, the wildness that returns in spring, the figure that became Robin Hood and the Wild Huntsman and the Lord of the Hunt and survives now as a tattoo on the arm of a hippie or a logo on a craft beer label. The voice claims him. *That too.*
