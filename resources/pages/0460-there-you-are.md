And this. *There you are.*

These three words may be the warmest in the poem. This is the voice of someone who has been waiting for you. Someone who was watching as you reassembled, breath by breath, limb by limb, and is now greeting you with quiet recognition. *There you are.* You have been found. You have arrived. You are visible to the one who was looking for you.

It is the voice of a parent finding a lost child in a crowd. It is the voice of a friend at a train station when you finally come down the platform. It is the voice of every reunion, condensed into three syllables. The universe has been waiting for you to come back to yourself, and now that you have, it is glad.
