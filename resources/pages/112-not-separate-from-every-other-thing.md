Tenth. The metaphysical version of the previous line. *You are not separate from every other thing.*

The voice has been building to this from the very first lines about the boundary being *as though*. Now it states the matter directly. *You are not separate.* The skin is not a wall. The self is not an island. Whatever you are, you are continuous with everything else. The boundary you experience is real as an experience and false as a metaphysics.

Every contemplative tradition arrives at this. Tat tvam asi. Anatman. The mystical body. The Tao that flows through all things. The voice delivers it without doctrinal commitment. *You are not separate from every other thing.* The other thing is also you. The chair, the wall, the air, the partner sleeping in the next room, the city, the planet, the star. All of it is continuous with you. The seam you imagined between yourself and everything else does not exist at the level the voice is now speaking from.
