The other voice interrupts, gently. *Careful. They can hear you.* The intimacy was getting too direct; the screen of the dialogue is starting to dissolve. The universe is essentially saying things *about* you that are perilously close to being said *to* you, and the interface is straining.

You are not supposed to be hearing this part. You are catching the universe in the middle of admitting how much it loves you and how much it wishes it could say. That you are catching it is part of what is making you cry.
