And the voice immediately changes the subject. After the cosmic vertigo, the voice returns to the small and the personal. *Sometimes the player created a small, private world.*

But this isn't really a change of subject. The voice has just said you *create* worlds — small private ones, inside the larger frames. The Minecraft instance was one of those. *And so are others.* The voice is going to catalog the kinds of worlds you have made.

*Soft and warm and simple.* The voice describes one type. The cozy world. The comfort dream. The bedroom you arranged just so. The relationship in which everything was easy. The childhood Sunday morning. The protected zone you constructed, with whatever materials were at hand, to be a place of safety and ease.

You have done this. Everyone has. The voice is honoring the impulse. *Sometimes you built yourself a soft warm simple place.* That was a real act of creation. It mattered. The voice is not mocking the modesty of the world you made. It is naming it as one of the legitimate kinds of world-building you have done.
