And the voice begins moving you backward in time.

Up to this point the voice has been telling you about *things you have dreamed*, in the present arrangement of your life. Now the voice is going to walk you backward through the chain of causes that produced you. You will be taken to your conception, then to your atomic origins in the heart of a dying star, then to the structure of the cosmos itself. The voice is going to give you the *full provenance* of what you are.

The simple imperative *let's go back* should remind you of someone walking you through a memory. A therapist. A storyteller. *Let's go back to the beginning.* The voice is offering to take you on a guided regression — not to a childhood trauma but to *the* beginning, the actual one, the one that started you. The journey is about to widen. The lens is about to pull back further than you have ever pulled it.
