The agreement. The voice begins to address you. The voice has turned. For the entire poem so far you have been *it* — third person, a topic of conversation. Now: *Player.* Second person. You are being looked at. The universe has shifted its gaze and is now facing you directly.

The ellipsis is a held breath. The voice is about to use your name and the other voice has to confirm.
