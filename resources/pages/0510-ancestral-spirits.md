The next layer. The dead are still here. Your grandmother's grandmother is still present in the line, still watching, still part of the household. And the animals carry spirits too — the bear, the eagle, the wolf, the salmon, the buffalo, the jaguar — each one a teacher, a kinsman, a power.

This is the religious world of most of human history. The boundary between living and dead is permeable. The boundary between human and animal is permeable. You are surrounded by presences. The voice says: *yes, those presences. Those were us. We have been the ancestors and the animals.*
