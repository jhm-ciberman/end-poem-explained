And again, the other voice cuts in.

Two shushes in close succession. The first stopped the recursion at *who created the universe*. This one stops the address at *you are the player, reading words…* The voice was about to say something it cannot say. Maybe it was going to tell you that the reading-itself is an act of cosmic significance. Maybe it was going to tell you that you are not really reading; that the words are reading *you*. Maybe it was going to fold the frame entirely. We do not get to know.

The shush is again merciful. The dream has limits. The voice is taking care of you by not saying what it almost said.

But the *almost* is its own message. The voice nearly broke through. The voice was *that close* to telling you something the universe usually keeps hidden. The fact that it almost did tells you that the something is *there*. There is a truth one layer above the words you are reading, and the voice has acknowledged it by gesturing toward it and then declining to deliver it.
