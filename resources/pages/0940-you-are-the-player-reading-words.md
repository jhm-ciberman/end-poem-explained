And then, mid-sentence, the voice turns directly to you.

*You are the player.* No longer *the player*. *You.* And what are you doing? *Reading words…*

The ellipsis. The voice trails off again. You can feel another revelation coming — the voice is about to tell you *what reading these words actually is*, what the words are doing to you, what is *happening* in this very moment as you take them in. The voice is about to break the frame in a way that would be enormous.
