Sixth. *You are the night.*

And the opposite. *You are also the night.* The dark, the cool, the quiet, the rest, the sleep, the unknown, the dream, the womb, the grave. All of these are also you. You are not just the bright active waking part. You are also the mysterious resting hidden part. The night is not the absence of you; the night is also you.

This is a small theological revolution. Most cultures associate the divine with light and the demonic with darkness. The voice refuses this. *You are the daylight and you are the night.* Both are sacred. Both are you. The universe is not picking sides between them. The wholeness includes the dark.
