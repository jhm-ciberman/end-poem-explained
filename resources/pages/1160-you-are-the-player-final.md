And then the voice points at you one last time.

*You are the player.* Not *the player was you*, past tense, story-distance. *You are the player.* Present tense. Right now. The one being addressed.

You are the player who was the universe who was love. The voice has just walked you through a complete cosmology and resolved it in two equations and now is reminding you that *you* are the term in the equations. All of it applies to you. The you in the bed. *You.*
