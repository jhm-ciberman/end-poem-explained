Permission granted. *Use its name.* The intimacy is being authorized. The voices agree that this moment requires the most personal possible form of address.

The ritual significance of speaking a name is vast across traditions. To know a name is to have a kind of relationship. To speak a name is to summon, to recognize, to commit. In the Hebrew Bible, naming is the first task given to the first human; the names that Adam gives the animals constitute them as known. In magical traditions, the true name of a thing is its essence. The universe is about to *name you* — to constitute you, in this moment, as fully known and fully addressed.
