The other voice's blessing. *Good.* The naming has happened. The connection is established. The work can proceed.

There is something Genesis-like about the *good* here. *And God saw that it was good.* The single syllable of cosmic approval. The universe has spoken your name and the speaking of it is *good* — meaning, the relation between you and the cosmos that has just been established by the naming is itself a good thing. Your existence in dialogue with the universe is endorsed.
