    text: "I will not tell the player how to live."

A vow. The voice makes it explicit. *I will not.* It draws the line and stays behind it. There will be no instruction manual. There will be no map. The universe declines, on principle, to take your life away from you by giving you the key to it.

This is, in its strange way, an enormous gift of respect. The universe trusts you to live your own life, even badly, even sadly, even confusedly, rather than reduce you to a follower of instructions. You are being honored by being left to figure it out. The silence is not abandonment. The silence is regard.
